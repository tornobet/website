FAQ

Do I lose my money?

Only if the result of the match does not match your prediction, you will lose the entire amount you bet. But if even a match is canceled, you can release all your money.

How do I place a bet?

To place a bet, you must first sign in with your crypto wallet (Metamask) and then follow the steps below: Browse the "Active Predictions" at the top of the home page. Enter the amount you want to bet on your chosen option. Tap the "Place Bet" button to submit a bet. Confirmation alert will be displayed after successful processing.

I have placed a bet by mistake. Can I ask for my bet to be cancelled?

Only if a sports match is cancelled, all bets are canceled and you can release your money. In addition, if someone on the opposite side does not make any bet with you, the bet is canceled and all the money can be released.

How do I know that I have won a placed bet?

All betting results can be seen on the "Dashboard". If it says "You won" in front of a record, you can release your amount plus the prize at the same time to your balance.

How can I report my problems and questions to the site?

You can connect with us through social networks and messengers listed at the bottom of the home page. You may also get answers to your questions and problems through our online support via "Crisp", if available.

How about the 30 minutes of wasted time calculating the result for the predictions?

No. The result of the match for football predictions is calculated up to the 90th minute along with a few minutes of wasted time. 30 minutes overtime is not counted.

How is the prize calculated?

If you predict a match correctly, All the amounts that the parties have bet, minus the percentage of the website's profit, will be awarded based on your share in the bet.
